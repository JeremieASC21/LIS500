About Issra Omer:

Issra Omer is a Product Director at Spotify, where she leads the Freemium experience and strategy. In this role, she helps decide what features free users can access and what is reserved for paid subscribers, a position that carries a lot of influence over how people interact with the platform. She also played a major role in bringing the lyrics feature to Spotify, something that millions of users rely on every day. Before joining Spotify, Omer worked at Meta and built her career in product management after studying at Stanford. Learning about her is important in a class on code, power, and intersectionality because her story shows how identity and leadership overlap in the tech world. As a woman of color in product management, she represents voices that are often missing in these spaces, and her work shows how product decisions can shape culture, access, and visibility on a massive scale. For us, we decided that Issra Omer is our favorite tech hero because we like what she stands for and how she uses her position to make technology more inclusive.
